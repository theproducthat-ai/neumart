## How to Use This Tree

1. Start from `business-goals/` — what are we trying to achieve?
2. Trace to `initiatives/` — what programme of work serves this goal?
3. Trace to `okrs/` — what's our quarterly commitment?
4. Trace to `features/` — what are we building?
5. Trace to `metrics/` — how are we measuring success?

Use this tree for:
- Prioritisation decisions ("does this feature support an OKR?")
- Leadership reporting ("are we making progress on our goals?")
- Post-release analysis ("did this feature move the OKR metric?")
