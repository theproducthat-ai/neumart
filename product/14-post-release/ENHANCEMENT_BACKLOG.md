# Enhancement Backlog

A list of improvement ideas, enhancement requests, and observations raised during UAT or post-release reviews that are not in scope for the current release. These are candidates for future sprints.

---

## How Items Are Added Here

Items arrive from:
- UAT feedback (type: Enhancement, Observation)
- Post-release review open items
- Product owner observation after release
- Customer feedback (if any feedback channel is active)

Items in this backlog must go through the standard request lifecycle (`/product-request`) before any development begins.

---

## How to Promote an Item

1. Product owner reviews backlog periodically (recommended: monthly).
2. To promote an item to active development, create a new REQ in `04-request-management/requests/` via `/product-request`.
3. Link the REQ to the backlog item.
4. Remove the item from this backlog once the REQ is created.
