## Engagement Levels

| Level | Meaning | Expected Engagement |
|---|---|---|
| `champion` | Active advocate, deeply involved | Sprint demo, weekly review |
| `active` | Regular engagement, provides input | Monthly stakeholder review |
| `informed` | Needs updates but not active | Monthly email update |
| `monitor` | Low priority, watch for changes | Quarterly |
