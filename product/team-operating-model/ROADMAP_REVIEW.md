# Roadmap Review

**Version**: 2.0  
**Owner**: Product Lead  
**Cadence**: Monthly (first week of each month), 90 minutes

---

## Purpose

The roadmap review is where the product roadmap is formally reviewed, updated, and communicated. It ensures the team is building the right things, that commitments are being met, and that leadership has visibility.

---

## Attendees

| Role | Attendance |
|---|---|
| Product Lead | Mandatory |
| Engineering Lead | Mandatory |
| Operations Lead | Mandatory |
| Business Owner / CTO | Mandatory |
| Designer | Recommended |
| Account Manager (if client commitments in scope) | Recommended |

---

## Pre-Work

**Product Lead**:
- Update the quarterly roadmap with actual progress
- Prepare a summary of what was delivered vs. committed last month
- Highlight any at-risk items for this month
- Propose any roadmap changes needed

**Engineering Lead**:
- Provide capacity update (actual vs. planned velocity)
- Flag any technical blockers affecting upcoming work

---

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 0-15 min | Last month: delivered vs. committed | Product Lead |
| 15-30 min | Roadmap health: at-risk items this quarter | Product Lead |
| 30-50 min | Proposed roadmap changes | Product Lead |
| 50-65 min | Capacity and dependency review | Engineering Lead |
| 65-80 min | Investment theme allocation check | Product Lead |
| 80-90 min | Decisions and actions | Product Lead |

---

## Roadmap Change Authority

Changes during this review:

| Change Type | Authority | Documentation |
|---|---|---|
| Reorder items within a quarter | Product Lead + Engineering Lead | Update roadmap document |
| Move item to next quarter | Product Lead + Business Owner | Update roadmap + notify stakeholders |
| Add a new item to current quarter | Product Lead + Business Owner | Only if capacity confirmed |
| Remove a client commitment | Business Owner + Client | Client communication required |
| Add a Strategic Initiative | Business Owner + CTO | Full intake and approval process |

All changes agreed in this meeting must be documented before the meeting ends — in the roadmap document and in affected object statuses.

---

## Outputs

Every roadmap review must produce:
- [ ] Updated roadmap document with actual delivery dates
- [ ] List of any changes made with rationale
- [ ] Client commitments status (on-track / at-risk / delayed)
- [ ] Communication plan if stakeholders need to be notified of changes
- [ ] Investment theme allocation — is it in line with targets?

---

## Roadmap Communication

After the monthly roadmap review:
1. Product Lead publishes a roadmap summary to the team (async)
2. If client commitments have changed: Account Manager notifies the client within 24 hours
3. If major changes: Business Owner presents to leadership
