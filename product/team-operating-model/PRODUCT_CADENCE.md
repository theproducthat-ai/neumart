# Product Cadence

**Version**: 2.0  
**Owner**: Product Lead

## Overview

This document is the master index of all recurring product meetings and reviews. Every team member should know which meetings they attend, when they happen, and what is expected of them.

## Sprint Structure

Neumart runs **2-week sprints** (10 working days).

```
Day 1  (Monday)    — Sprint Planning + Weekly Product Review
Day 2  (Tuesday)   — Development
Day 3  (Wednesday) — Design Review
Day 4  (Thursday)  — QA/UAT Review
Day 5  (Friday)    — Development
Day 6  (Monday)    — Development + Weekly Product Review
Day 7  (Tuesday)   — Development
Day 8  (Wednesday) — Design Review
Day 9  (Thursday)  — QA/UAT Review + Feature Freeze
Day 10 (Friday)    — Sprint Demo + Retrospective + Release
```

_Note: Feature freeze = no new feature work merges after Day 9. Bug fixes only._

---

## Principles

1. **Meetings are decisions, not updates** — information can be shared async; meetings are for decisions
2. **Every meeting has an agenda** — no agenda, no meeting
3. **Every meeting produces outcomes** — decisions made, actions assigned with owners and due dates
4. **Async first** — weekly product review runs from a pre-circulated dashboard; attendees read before arriving
