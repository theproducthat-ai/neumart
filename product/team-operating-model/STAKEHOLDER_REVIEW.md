# Stakeholder Review

**Version**: 2.0  
**Owner**: Product Lead / Operations Lead  
**Cadence**: Monthly, 60 minutes

---

## Purpose

The monthly stakeholder review keeps business stakeholders informed of product progress, surfaces their priorities, and confirms that product delivery aligns with business needs. It is not a decision-making meeting — decisions happen through the roadmap review and product review cadences.

---

## Attendees

| Role | Attendance |
|---|---|
| Product Lead | Mandatory |
| Operations Lead | Mandatory |
| Business Owner / CTO | Mandatory |
| Account Manager (if applicable) | Recommended |
| Sales or Commercial Lead (if applicable) | Recommended |
| Engineering Lead | Optional — joins if technical context is needed |

---

## Pre-Work

**Product Lead**:
- Prepare a one-page progress summary (what was delivered this month)
- Prepare roadmap forward look (next 4-6 weeks)
- Highlight any roadmap changes made since last review
- Flag any items where stakeholder input is needed

---

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 0-10 min | What we shipped this month | Product Lead |
| 10-20 min | What's coming in the next 4-6 weeks | Product Lead |
| 20-35 min | Stakeholder priorities: any new inputs, commitments, or concerns? | Operations Lead |
| 35-50 min | Open discussion: questions, feedback | All |
| 50-60 min | Actions and next steps | Product Lead |

---

## Rules for Stakeholder Reviews

1. **Stakeholders can raise priorities here, but cannot add them to the sprint directly** — requests enter through the formal intake process
2. **Be specific about trade-offs** — if a stakeholder wants something added urgently, explain what would need to move to accommodate it
3. **Commitments made in this meeting must be documented** — any commitment to a stakeholder becomes a `CLIENT_COMMITMENT` object and enters the roadmap
4. **Do not make commitments you haven't confirmed with Engineering Lead**

---

## Outputs

Every stakeholder review must produce:
- [ ] Summary of what was delivered sent to stakeholders within 24 hours
- [ ] Any new requests submitted through the intake process
- [ ] Any commitments made documented as client commitment objects
- [ ] Any roadmap changes agreed in principle noted for the next roadmap review

---

## Client-Specific Reviews

For named clients with active commitments, a separate client review may be scheduled:
- Format: same structure but client-specific
- Operations Lead and Account Manager own the relationship
- Product Lead presents delivery progress
- Any gaps in commitments communicated proactively, never reactively
