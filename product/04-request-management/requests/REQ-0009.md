## Request Summary

Two visual adjustments to the product card on the product listing page:

1. **Image size reduction** — The product card image is currently too large. It should be reduced so more card content (name, price) is visible without scrolling, and the card proportions look tighter on the listing grid.
2. **Price-name alignment** — The product price should be visually aligned with the product name, so both elements share the same left edge and the card information hierarchy feels consistent.

---

## Business / UX Ask

The product card images dominate the card height on the listing page. Reducing the image height will allow a tighter card that shows more products per viewport and improves scannability. The price label should feel anchored under the product name, not floating or offset.
