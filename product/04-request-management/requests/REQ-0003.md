## Request Summary

The product card layout should be updated so that the product price is visually aligned with the product name, creating a tighter and more scannable information hierarchy. Currently the name, quantity, and price are separated vertically in a way that disconnects the price from the product title.

---

## Business / UX Ask

The product price should visually align with the product name so the information hierarchy is cleaner and easier to scan. The price should feel tightly connected with the product title, not separated from it by vertical distance.

---

## Technical Scope

- No data, schema, or business logic change.
- Layout and spacing change only — CSS / Tailwind class adjustment on the product card component.
- The product name and product price should start from the same left edge with consistent alignment.
- Scope is limited to the product card on SCR-CUS-0001 (Product Listing). No other screens confirmed in scope.
