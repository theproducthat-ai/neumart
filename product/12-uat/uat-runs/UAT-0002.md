## Expected Outcomes

The product owner expects:

1. Opening `/products` shows product cards where the heart/favourite icon sits visually on top of the product image at the top-right corner — not below the image in the action row.
2. The bottom action area of every card has a single full-width "Add to cart" button. It looks cleaner and less cluttered than before.
3. Tapping the heart icon behaves exactly as before (add/remove favourite; prompt to sign in if signed out).
4. The change looks correct on both mobile and desktop.
