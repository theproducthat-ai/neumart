## Expected Outcomes

Before running UAT, the product owner's definition of a passing result:

1. The carousel looks clean and professional — comparable to Blinkit or BigBasket in style.
2. All 5 placeholder banners are visible and communicate grocery-relevant promotions.
3. Auto-scroll works naturally without being jarring or too fast.
4. Clicking a banner takes the customer to the correct category.
5. The rest of the product listing page (chips, search, grid) works exactly as before.
6. On mobile, the experience is smooth without arrows cluttering the UI.

---

## Business Feedback

> Carousel looks clean and professional. Auto-scroll and navigation work well. Mobile experience is smooth. Placeholder images to be replaced before production go-live. Click-through destinations deferred to a future enhancement — acceptable for this release. Overall storefront quality is significantly improved.

## Decision

**UAT Decision:** Conditional Pass

**Business Owner Statement:**

> The carousel feature is approved for release. The implementation meets the core business objective of adding a professional promotional display to the product listing page. Conditions: (1) replace placeholder images with production promotional images before go-live — this is a config-level change with no code required. (2) Banner click-throughs will be implemented in a future enhancement.
