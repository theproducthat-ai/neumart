## Why Two Folders Existed

During initial V2 setup, two reference folders were created with overlapping scope:
- `references/` — originally scoped to external research, competitors, regulatory documents
- `reference-materials/` — scoped to PDFs, spreadsheets, meeting recordings, research reports, Figma links, emails, Excel files

These have been consolidated. `reference-materials/` is the source of truth.

## What Belongs in reference-materials/

- PDFs, spreadsheets, meeting recordings, research reports
- Competitor analysis documents
- Regulatory and compliance documents (e.g., WCAG, PCI)
- Client emails, contracts, and briefs that inform product decisions
- Industry reports and benchmarks
- Figma links (use `figma-links/` for Figma-specific objects)
- Flow diagrams (use `flow-diagrams/` for flow diagram objects)
- Screenshots (use `screen-shots/` for screenshot references)

## Legacy Objects

If any `REF-XXXX` objects were ever created in this folder, they remain here as read-only. Do not create new objects here.
