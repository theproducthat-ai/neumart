# Strategy

**Object type**: `strategy`  
**Owner**: Product Lead / CEO  
**Note**: Strategy objects — documents that define strategic direction for the product or a product area. Longer-horizon than OKRs; more specific than business goals.

## When to Use

- A go-to-market strategy for a new product area
- A platform strategy document
- A competitive positioning document
- A technology strategy (e.g., platform migration approach)

## Relationship to Other Objects

`business-goals/` → what we want to achieve  
`strategy/` → how we plan to achieve it  
`okrs/` → what we're doing this quarter to execute the strategy  
`initiatives/` → the programmes of work that execute the strategy

## Format

`STR-[AREA]-NNNN.md` with fields:
`id`, `title`, `area`, `time_horizon`, `context`, `approach`, `key_choices`, `risks`, `owner`, `created_date`
