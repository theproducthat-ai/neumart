## Purpose

The business stakeholder view answers the question business stakeholders most care about: "Is my request being worked on, when will it be delivered, and what is the product doing for the business right now?"

---

## How to Submit a New Request

1. Contact the Product Lead directly or via the team's intake channel
2. Product Lead will create a request object and send confirmation within 2 business days
3. You will receive updates when the request moves to approved, in-progress, and delivered

_Full intake process: `product/os/policies/INTAKE_SOURCE_RULES.md`_
