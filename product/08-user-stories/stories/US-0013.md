## User Story

```
As a Customer on a mobile device,
I want to swipe left or right on the banner carousel to navigate between banners,
So that I can browse all promotions using the natural touch gesture I expect on mobile.
```

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] **AC-1 (Swipe left advances):**
  Given the customer is viewing the carousel on a mobile device,
  When the customer swipes left across the carousel,
  Then the carousel advances to the next banner.

- [ ] **AC-2 (Swipe right goes back):**
  Given the customer is viewing the carousel on a mobile device,
  When the customer swipes right across the carousel,
  Then the carousel goes back to the previous banner.

- [ ] **AC-3 (Swipe wraps at ends):**
  Given the customer swipes left on the last banner,
  When the swipe completes,
  Then the carousel wraps to the first banner.
  And given the customer swipes right on the first banner,
  When the swipe completes,
  Then the carousel wraps to the last banner.

- [ ] **AC-4 (Short swipe not registered):**
  Given the customer touches and slightly moves their finger on the carousel (less than 50px horizontal movement),
  When the touch ends,
  Then the carousel does not advance — the gesture is not registered as a swipe.

- [ ] **AC-5 (Swipe resets auto-scroll timer):**
  Given auto-scroll is active,
  When the customer completes a swipe,
  Then the auto-scroll timer resets and does not immediately advance to the next banner after the swipe.

- [ ] **AC-6 (Vertical scroll not blocked):**
  Given the customer is scrolling the page vertically over the carousel area,
  When the scroll is primarily vertical (vertical movement > horizontal movement),
  Then the page scrolls normally and the carousel does not intercept the gesture.

- [ ] **AC-7 (Dot indicators update on swipe):**
  Given the customer swipes to banner 2,
  When the swipe completes,
  Then dot 2 becomes the active dot.

## Business Rules

| # | Rule |
|---|---|
| BR-1 | Minimum swipe distance: 50px horizontal movement to register as an intentional swipe |
| BR-2 | Swipe direction is determined by net horizontal displacement (touchEnd.x − touchStart.x) |
| BR-3 | Vertical scroll must not be blocked by the carousel swipe handler |
| BR-4 | Swipe resets the auto-scroll timer (same as arrow click) |
